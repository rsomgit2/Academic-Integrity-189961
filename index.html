Academic Integrity

Course Description

This course is meant to help you be successful and show your full abilities. It is designed to achieve the following goals: It's a refresher on academic writing, exam expectations, working in groups, and submitting successful assignments at the graduate level at the University of Toronto. It aims to empower you to become a better decision maker when it comes to issues of academic integrity and professional conduct in general. It introduces you to a wide range of resources at Rotman, the UofT, and beyond. We designed this course to protect you from difficulties later in your degree program, when the consequences of slipping up can be very severe. With or without this course, “I did not know” is simply not a defense that the University can ever accept when it comes to academic integrity…
